About us

College Buzz is a centralized platform that simplifies college communication, providing instant notifications, event registrations, and seamless management for colleges and students College Buzz is an innovative college event and notification platform designed to keep students and college administrations connected in a fast, organized, and professional manner. It eliminates the chaos of scattered messages in WhatsApp groups by providing a centralized hub where students can find all important updates, events, and notices in one place. Whether it's event registrations, exam schedules, placement opportunities, or campus activities,
College Buzz ensures that students never miss out on important updates, while colleges can easily manage and promote
events through a dedicated admin panel.


Why College Buzz(What it solve)


Currently, most colleges rely on WhatsApp groups, emails, or notice boards to communicate important information. This leads to:
Information Overload: Important updates get lost among casual conversations.

  Missed Deadlines: Students forget about event registrations or exam notifications.

  Lack of Organization: Searching for old messages is time-consuming and inefficient.

  Unprofessional Communication: Colleges lack a structured way to notify students.
